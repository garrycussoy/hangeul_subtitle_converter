What is Hangeul Subtitle Converter?

Hangeul Subtitle Converter is an app which let you watch Korean youtube video with Korean Romanization subtitle. This app works by converting Hangeul subtitle into Korean Romanization subtitle. When you play the video, instead of showing Hangeul subtitle, it will show Korean Romanization subtitle.

Why Use Hangeul Subtitle Converter?

As we know, many Korean youtube videos have Korean subtitle, but only in Hangeul version. Of course, to be able to read it, you need to know Hangeul symbols first. Sometimes, you want to just read it, for example, if it a song and you want to sing along the video. You then end up opening new tab to search for the lyrics in Romanization version. Therefore, you cannot enjoy the video, since you focus on reading the lyrics in other tab. What a nightmare! 🙁 Fortunately, Hangeul Subtitle Converter come to the rescue! 😊 You don't need to search for the lyrics anymore. Instead, this app will play your video while replacing the Hangeul subtitle verison into Romanization version. So now, you can sing along the video while enjoying watching the video. 😎

What to Do?

Go to youtube first and find yourself a video that you want to watch. Copy the link (either full URL or just the video ID, both will work) and then paste it to the input box below. Click 'Watch Now!' and enjoy the video!

Note
You need video with Korean subtitle to make it works (of course, if not, what's the point of using this app? 😆  ). The subtitle need to be uploaded by author of the video, not the one automatically generated by youtube.
